About

ByteSynergy

ByteSynergy, is a 54 hour long hackathon conducted by the students of IIIT Bangalore as a part of Synergy - The Techfest of IIITB. It is a chance to put your creative minds against some of the best student developers in our country.
Hack away and bring your ideas to fruition, using any techstack, language, or open source codebase! Make apps that will not only contribute to the world of open source development, but also help people improve their quality of life.

Your ByteSynergy, More magnificent this year!

Enjoyed our last iteration? Well this year there is a lot more fun, it's going to be a month long hackathon!
Yes, you read that right. This long duration will help you build robust hacks of real value!

FAQs

Who all can participate? Are there any pre-requisites?

All students currently enrolled in an undergraduate or postgraduate degree program are welcome to participate in the hackathon.
Although prior experience in coding will be an advantage, the teams also require a great idea and amazing presentation skills to win. If you aren't experienced, this hackathon will be a great place to begin!


What is the maximum team size?

The maximum team size allowed is four, but smaller teams are also welcome.


Is there a participation fee for the hackathon?

Participating in this hackathon is absolutely free! Just join in and have fun!

Do I need a team to participate?

You can participate alone! If you want to find any teammates, we will try our best to match you with someone!


Should all the team members be from the same college?

Not at all, you just need an idea to get started!


How is the hackathon going to be conducted?

The hackathon will span a total of 1 month and is going to be completely online.

Tracks

The 4 tracks

Blockchain Development

Buld creative DApps to make the world future ready with blockchain.

Healthcare and Social Issues

We are all surrounded by countless number of issues, be it social such as simple parking space issues to more complex such as healthcare.. everything could use an improvement. So let your imaginations flow and contribute something to the society.

Game Jam

Hackers work to conceptualize, design, and build a functioning version of one or more game projects over a month.

Open innovation

It is time to get your brain juices flowing! This track is one where you can express the full extent of your creativity. Anything from game-dev to an android or web app, Augmented Reality or any other field that interests you. There are no limits to your creativity and we look forward to seeing your extraordinary creations!
